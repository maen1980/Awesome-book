Awesome Books

By Maen and Adrian

This is our take over the first project from 2nd module. We had to do a webpage that will store books locally. We implemented the add and remove function so you can always remove old books and add new ones. Awesome Books is an open, editable library catalog, building towards a web page for every book ever published. Just like Wikipedia, you can contribute new information or corrections to the catalog. You can browse by subjects, authors or lists members have created. If you love books, why not help build a library?
